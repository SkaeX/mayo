Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is MAYO?

Mentor-A-YOuth (MAYO) is a social innovation project aiming to empower youths by matching them with suitable mentors in their chosen career interests. We are building a community of people from all works of life to come together and support our mentees in advancing their careers.

Who is a mentee?

Anyone (youth) who needs mentorship.

Who is a mentor?

A mentor is someone who:

  • Is willing to sacrifice 2 hours in a month to communicate to mentee.
  • Has at least 5 years of continuing professional progress after graduation
  • Can at least mentor one person within 5 months
  • Agree to 5 months commitment to the programme

How long does the mentoring period last?

A mentoring match will last for 5 months.

Is there an application period?

Yes for our mentees, and no for our mentors. Always watch out for call for applications. Mentors are always welcome to sign up.
